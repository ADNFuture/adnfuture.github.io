For the community

Dear ADN,

When the founders of App.net published the State of the Union post, it came as a shock to many of us. Though they assured us that App.net will continue to run in its current form indefinitely, there is no hope that it will grow any further. It is unlikely, for example, that we will ever see new features.

Though users haven't yet left in droves, App.net is no longer seen as a viable service. People have begun to move on and more will undoubtedly follow. We believe that App.net's greatest feature is its incredible community. This is something worth preserving.

These are our goals

  • We want to preserve the community that has flourished.
  • We want to grow the community and attract new members.
  • We want to enhance the service with new features.
  • We want to actively listen to, and communicate with this community.

And we're going to start right now.

By the community

Let's do this together

The future of this network cannot be decided by developers alone.

Every member of this community is welcome to chime in on our Trello boards; where members from our community have already submitted ideas for features, names, and marketing, for example.

But that is not all. We will form an Advisory Panel which will have five members — users who are explicitly not developers. This panel will take an active role in the development of the service.

If you'd like to apply for a position on the Advisory Panel, please sign up for our mailing list below and check the "Yes, I want to help!" box. We will contact you soon.

Of the community

Ideals

We still believe in the ideals upon which App.net was founded. Whatever we create will also be a place where you own your data and where you are the customer.

The next logical step

App.net was a great step in the right direction: a company not interested in selling out its users to advertisers. But ultimately, its goals did not match the expectations of its community. Thus the next logical step is a service that is community-run. This is the only way to ensure that the community's needs will be addressed.
